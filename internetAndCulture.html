How the Internet has redefined culture:

For most of human history, the spread of culture has been confined by several factors:

  • Geographical boundaries
  • Political barriers
  • Language and cultural differences
The spread of the internet around the world however has allowed an unprecedented transfer of cultural and societal knowledge around the globe,
leading to the creation of a foundational 'global culture' that transcends geographical and political boundaries.
